Bio

Matej Smutný is a Slovak singer songwriter and producer blending atmospheric pop with emotional storytelling. He began as a percussionist and composer and entered the scene as a solo artist in 2020 with his debut single Psycho Love which quickly gained international attention and reached MTV rotations across Europe. Since then he has released the EP Van Stories and his debut album Aura building a strong presence across Slovakia the Czech Republic and beyond. His music regularly appears on radio while his live shows range from intimate acoustic sets to full band performances across Europe including a sold out show in London. Currently based in Sweden Matej is working on new music and international projects while continuing to shape his sound and artistic identity.
