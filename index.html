matej smutny

Bio

Matej Smutný is a Slovak singer songwriter and producer connecting atmospheric pop with emotional storytelling. Originally trained as a percussionist and composer, he has performed across Europe and the United States both as a touring drummer and as a solo artist.

Over the years, Matej has collaborated with artists and creatives across multiple countries, shaping a sound that moves between intimate songwriting and cinematic production. He entered the solo scene in 2020 with his debut single Psycho Love, which gained international attention and reached MTV rotations across Europe.

Currently based in Sweden, Matej continues to develop his artistic identity through new releases and international projects, connecting different influences into a distinct and evolving sound.
